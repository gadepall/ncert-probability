Let 
\begin{align}
	 X =
	\begin{cases}
		1, & \text{if number is even}\\ 
		0, & \text{if number is odd}
	\end{cases}
\end{align}
Then  
\begin{align}
               p_X\brak{1} &= \frac{50}{100}
               =\frac{1}{2}
	       \\
               p_X\brak{0} &= \frac{50}{100}
               =\frac{1}{2}      
\end{align}
and $X \sim \text{Ber}\brak{\frac{1}{2}}$.
