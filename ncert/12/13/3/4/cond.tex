The probability that the student knows the answer and he answered it correctly is 
%
\begin{align}
	\pr{X=1|Y=1} &=\frac{\pr{Y=1|X=1}\pr{X=1}}{\sum_{i=0}^{1}Pr(Y=1|X=i)Pr(X=i)}\\
	&=\frac{0.75}{{0.25\times0.25}+{1\times0.75}}
	=0.92308
	\end{align}
