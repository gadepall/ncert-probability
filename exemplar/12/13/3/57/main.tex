Question: If $\pr{AB}=\frac{7}{10}$ and $\pr{B}=\frac{17}{20}$, then $\pr{A|B}$ equals
\begin{enumerate}
\item $\frac{14}{17}$
\item $\frac{17}{20}$
\item $\frac{7}{8}$
\item $\frac{1}{8}$
\end{enumerate}
\fi 
\solution
We know
\begin{align}
\pr{A|B}&=\frac{\pr{AB}}{\pr{B}}\\
&=\frac{\frac{7}{10}}{\frac{17}{20}}\\
&=\frac{14}{17}
\end{align}
