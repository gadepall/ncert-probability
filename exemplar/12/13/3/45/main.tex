From the given information,
\begin{align}
\pr{X=0} = \pr{Z=0} 
         &= \frac{1}{3} \\
\pr{X=1} = \pr{Z=1} 
         &= \frac{2}{3}\\
\pr{ Y=0 | X=0} &= \frac{3}{7} \\
\pr{ Y=0 | X=1} &= \frac{4}{7}     
\end{align}
Hence, the desierd probability is
\begin{align}
\pr{Y=0} &= \pr{X=0} \times \pr{ Y=0 | {X=0}} + \pr{X=1} \times \pr{ Y=0 | {X=1}} \\
                                 &= \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{3}{7} + \frac{2}{3} \times \frac{4}{7} 
                                 = \frac{7}{21} 
\end{align}
