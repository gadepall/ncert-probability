Since 26 out of 52 playing cards are red, 
\begin{align}
         p_{X_1}\brak{k} = \frac{26}{52}= \frac{1}{2} \quad \cbrak{k=0,1}
\end{align} 
Also, 
\begin{align}
	p_{X_2|X_1}\brak{i|j} 
			= 
			\begin{cases}
				\frac{25}{51} & i = j \\
                         \frac{26}{51}& i \ne j \\
			\end{cases}
\end{align}
The desired probability is
\begin{align}
\pr{X_1 \ne X_2}= \sum_{i=0}^{1}\pr{X_1 \ne X_2|X_1=i}p_{X}\brak{i}
                           =\frac{26}{51}
\end{align}
