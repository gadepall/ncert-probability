Thus, the pmf of $X$ is
\begin{align}
    p_X(k) = 
    \begin{cases}
        \frac{3}{6} &  k = -1\\
        \frac{2}{6} &  k = 1\\
        \frac{1}{6} &  k = 4
    \end{cases}
\end{align}\\
The expected profit is
\begin{align}
    E\brak{X} &= \sum_{k} k p_X(k) \\
    &= \frac{3}{6}\brak{-1} + \frac{2}{6}\brak{1} + \frac{1}{6}\brak{4}\\
    &= \frac{1}{2}
\end{align}
